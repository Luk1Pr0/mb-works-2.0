Who are we

MB Works is a team of friendly professionals, with over 10 years of experience in the construction and building industry. Working in close partnership with Platinum Property Services , we provide high quality services for a great value, to all of our customers. We provide you with a free quotation!

Our main focus is on building, refurbishing, and repairing all kinds of properties. Based in Slough, we offer services within London, Beaconsfield, Maidenhead, Reading and more. If you have any questions or a project that you need assistance with, get in touch now!
